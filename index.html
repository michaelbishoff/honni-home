How to use Honni

Mission

Honni connects consumers and buyers with farmers, growers, and sellers. The health-conscious consumer selects any produce they are looking for, and our site immediately locates farmers and backyard growers within a certain radius which sell exactly what the buyer wants.

Modeled off of Uber and Airbnb, Honni is a peer-to-peer service, meaning even a small time community-gardener can sell their produce through the app once they get verified.
